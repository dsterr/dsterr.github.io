What is Vandra?

Vandra comes from the Swedish word meaning “to wander” - a quiet, purposeful kind of exploration. It’s not about drifting aimlessly; it’s about moving through the world with curiosity, intention, and openness. That idea sits at the heart of this website.

The name reflects a personal ethic: a belief in learning by doing, discovering new perspectives, and staying curious about how things work. Whether it’s activities, creative projects, writing, or practical resources, everything here is shaped by that same spirit of exploration. Vandra is a place to follow paths, try ideas, and see where they lead.

The branding reinforces this theme.

The path in the logo represents movement, growth, and the willingness to take the next step - even when the destination isn’t fully known. The arrow‑shaped A points upward, symbolising progress and direction. Together, they create a visual story about journeying, learning, and moving forward.

The colour palette draws from nature: muted greens, soft neutrals, and gentle contrasts. These tones echo the outdoors - calm, grounded, and organic - and connect back to the values behind the name. They’re intentionally understated, giving the site a sense of clarity and space while keeping the focus on the content.

Vandra isn’t just a title; it’s a framework for how the site works and what it aims to offer: a place to explore ideas, share resources, and build things that help others do the same.

Key Values

These values guide the purpose, tone, and direction of Vandra, shaping how the site is built and what it aims to share.

Curiosity

A commitment to asking questions, trying new things, and staying open to learning - whether through activities, writing, or creative projects.

Exploration

The belief that progress comes from wandering a little: following paths, testing ideas, and discovering what works along the way.

Growth

The upward arrow in the logo reflects personal and practical development - improving skills, expanding knowledge, and building confidence.

Clarity

A clean, calm design and structured content that make information easy to navigate and understand.

Nature

The colour palette and visual style draw from natural tones, grounding the site in themes of balance, calm, and authenticity.
